# Privacy Policy – Budget Calculator

**Last updated:** Version 1.0

## English

Budget Calculator respects your privacy.

This application does **not collect, store, transmit, or share** any personal or sensitive user data.

### Data Collection
- No personal information is collected
- No user accounts
- No analytics or tracking
- No third-party services
- No advertisements

### Data Storage
All data entered in the app (such as expenses and budget calculations) is stored **locally on the user’s device only** and is never transmitted externally.

### Changes
If the app’s behavior changes in the future regarding data usage, this policy will be updated accordingly.
